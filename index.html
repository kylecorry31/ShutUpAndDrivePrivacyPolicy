Shut Up & Drive!

Privacy Policy

User Data Policy

I do not collect any of your information - none of the information required by the app is sent to a server (the app doesn't even have a server!).

Shut Up & Drive! requires the phone and contact permissions to detect when you are receiving a phone call and then read out the name of the contact who is calling for you to hear.

Shut Up & Drive! uses the activity recognition and location permissions to determine if you are driving, so it can automatically enable driving mode.

Shut Up & Drive! also uses SMS permissions to send auto reply texts to people who may message you while driving.

All features which use these permission can be toggled on or off, and none will be in use while Shut Up & Drive! is not activated.
